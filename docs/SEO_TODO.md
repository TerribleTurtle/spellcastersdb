# SEO Content Roadmap

This document tracks future content ideas to capture high-value search traffic.

## High Priority (Launch Prep)

- [ ] **Titan Breakdown Guide**
  - **Concept:** "Deep Dive" into Titan mechanics (Summoning, Gauge, Strategy).
  - **Why:** Unique mechanic = Confusion = High Search Volume.
  - **Keywords:** "How to summon Titan", "Titan Gauge Guide", "Best Titan Spellcasters".

- [ ] **Founder's Pack Value Guide**
  - **Concept:** Breakdown of Explorer vs. Pioneer vs. Architect packs.
  - **Why:** High transactional intent ("Is the Architect pack worth it?").
  - **Keywords:** "Spellcasters Chronicles Founder Pack Worth It", "Founder Pack Comparison".

## Medium Priority (Post-Launch)

- [ ] **"Meta Snapshot" Weekly Posts**
  - **Concept:** Automated or semi-automated blog posts ranking the top used units/spells.
  - **Why:** Drives recurring traffic and community engagement.

- [ ] **New Player Guide**
  - **Concept:** "From Zero to Hero" - Basic mechanics explanation.
  - **Why:** Captures "Spellcasters Chronicles Guide" broad keywords.
